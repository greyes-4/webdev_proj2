Volleyball

Volleyball is a game played by two teams, with each team having six players. Players use their hands to hit a ball over a high net. A point is scored when the ball touches the court within the opponent's playing area. To prevent being scored on, a player can hit the ball up toward a teammate before the ball touches the ground. The second player can either hit the ball over the net or pass it to a third teammate, who can then hit it across the net. Only three touches are allowed before the ball must be returned over the net.

Tips and Tricks

How to Serve

An underhand serve is one of the safest and most basic ways to get the ball over the net. First, ensure you're behind the service line, with your non-dominant foot slightly forward. Hold the ball at thigh-height with your non-dominant hand and make a fist with your dominant hand. Bring your dominant arm back, step forward with your dominant foot, and swing your arm to make contact with the ball. Aim to hit the ball at an angle that gives it good height while moving forward.

How to Pass

For passing, you want to make contact with the ball using the lower part of your arms. Place one hand on top of the other with both palms facing up, then bring your thumbs together at the tips of your fingers. Extend your arms to create a solid platform for passing. To achieve a high pass, use your legs to push through the ball and move to position yourself comfortably before making contact.
